Come build public good communications with us

Relational is a fun lab full of clever people experimenting with communications tech.

The internet has fundamentally changed the nature of how we communicate, and as a result how we commune, convene, cooperate and collaborate. The root of human culture, our digitally mediated socialization is a shared human interest across market, state, religion, and everywhere in between.

Relational is located at this crossroads, a place designed for social builders from myriad backgrounds: technologists, communications artists, and social workers. Here we agree to find common ground to allow for a productive mixing of shared interest in developing public good digital communications products.

Our core offering is a fresh approach to teaching and learning how to build digital comms public goods. Designed as a kind of Montessori school for social builders, Relational is a peer-to-peer practice-based milieu.
