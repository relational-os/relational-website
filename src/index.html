Relational is a way of work

and a community of practice

for social technologists

Who we are

From the engine room tinkering with database design out into the world to playtest our creations, we are a diverse group of high-agency individuals working across the tech continuum

What we do

Creating software products that are as playful as they are useful, we find elegant and creative solutions to complex social problems.

How we operate

Through playful, participatory and build-centric practices and rituals, we make cross-functional collaboration fun.

Our social practices are open-source, peer-to-peer, modular, and designed for a diversity of builders to experiment, create, and ship.

Why we do it

By actively exploring the ethical, spiritual, and philosophical conundrums of social tech, we are in better relationship with ourselves, each other and the world.
