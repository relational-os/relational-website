We build for communities in service of better connection

All of our products start as projects and grow out of a shared need we identify within the Relational community. We're focused on explorations into different communications challenges: how can we build better digital products to improve human connection?

We start by building for ourselves using our social-first approach, if it works for us on a smaller scale, we begin to expand our reach. Our entrepreneurial spirit guides us to directly serve our community and neighboring communities.

The form of our projects depends on the context and the builders. We can create just about every type of product: from tools, to games, to protocols. So long as our offering serves to better connect people, it's fair game.
