We build for communities in service of better connection

We’re in the business of communal goods and services. Our customer is the community, so all of our projects are explorations into how can we better collaborate, cohere, coordinate.

The form of our projects depends on the context and the builders. We can create just about anything: from tools, to games, to protocols. And we employ the latest technical innovations from blockchain to AI/ML, to XR, to network tech.

Sometimes our projects don’t scale, that’s ok. We’re more interested in building-to-understand new modalities of digital connection than we are in bringing a whiz-bang product to market to get rich.

When we do decide to scale a product and bring it to market, we tend towards open-source when possible. Especially for the patterns of connection we discover, these are not something to hoard but something to share freely.
